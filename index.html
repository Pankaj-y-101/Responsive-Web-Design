Future Of Online Education:
Being able to learn online provides you with the freedom to learn wherever you are, without having to attend a class at an allocated time. Make education accessible- Online learning gives you access to education later on in life, for example if you work full-time but want to pursue an area of study.

Need Of Online Education:
Whether it's due to lack of teacher, internet access, limited resources, or less percieved interest on the part of student -- or all three -- these issues are holding schools back from achieving the equality in education that all student deserve.Rural schools, high schools tend to struggle the most when it comes to providing a broad catalog of course option.


Meeting the challenges Head-On:
By providing all student access to high-quality college-and career ready curriculum, certified teacher, and up-to-date instructional material and tools-- including computer and related technology -- rural schools can more adequately provide their students with the resources they need to be prepare in today's ever-changing world.

Teacher Shortage:
Online learning helps schools overcome teacher shortage.As online learning continues to evolve, it's peoducing excellent outcomes for all schools.The internet allows for access to high quality teacher to unencumbered by geography or demographics. Online learning is not only a good option for teacher shortage issues solely because of the lack of alternatives.
